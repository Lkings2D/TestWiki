Welcome to Halmgaard Wiki

Halmgaard is an immersive MMO developed by Gruset, heavily inspired by the classic MMORPG Tibia and similar games.

Set in a vast, open world, Halmgaard offers players the freedom to explore diverse environments, engage in strategic combat, and embark on epic quests. With a focus on community interaction and challenging gameplay, players can form alliances, discover hidden treasures, and build their legacy in a world full of danger and adventure.

Whether you're battling fierce monsters, mastering skills, or uncovering ancient secrets, Halmgaard promises a nostalgic yet fresh experience for all MMO enthusiasts.

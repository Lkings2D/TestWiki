Nomad Bladelancer

A powerful warrior skilled in hand-to-hand combat.

Type Nomad Bladelancer
Location Desert Nomad's Camp

Overview

Nomad Bladelancers can be found in the Desert Nomad's Camp.

Hardened by the desert sun, these elite warriors serve as the vanguard of the Nomad tribes, excelling in speed and close combat.

Combat Notes

  • Nomad Bladelancers are fast melee enemies with high physical resistance.
  • Fire or poison-based attacks are especially effective.
  • They often spawn in small groups near the Nomad tents, protecting key chests and loot.

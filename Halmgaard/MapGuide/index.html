Mob Hunting Locations

Below are detailed hunting location guides for each major mob. Click the video thumbnails to watch routes and cave access guides created by community members.

Orcs

Recommended Level: 0–10

A beginner-friendly area to start your combat journey. Features small groups of Orcs near town—ideal for early training and basic loot.

Cyclops

Recommended Level: 10–20

Hidden cave containing Cyclops and Behemoths. Beware: the area is larger than it seems, and several routes connect deeper into the mountains.

Bandits

Recommended Level: 20–25

Bandits lurk near remote outposts and ruined structures. Expect melee-heavy enemies with moderate loot drops.

Goblins

Recommended Level: 25–30

Dense forest camps filled with Goblins. Quick respawns and mid-tier loot make this an efficient grinding area.

Dwarves, Behemoths & Demonic Behemoths

Recommended Levels:

• Dwarves – 30+
• Behemoths – 60+
• Demonic Behemoth – 60+

A dangerous network of tunnels and caverns beneath the mountains. Ideal for group hunting or high-defense solo players.

Vampires

Recommended Level: 40+

Ancient crypts and dark caves populated by Vampires and Minotaurs. High HP enemies with good loot potential and strong magic attacks.

Dragons

Recommended Level: 50+

The ultimate mid-game challenge. Explore the Abyss, Sparkborn, and Glacial regions where Dragons dwell.

Swamp Spiders & Pirates

Recommended Level: 60+

Swamp Spiders and Pirate Islands offer mixed physical and poison threats. Good for experienced adventurers seeking challenge and valuable loot.

Hydras

Recommended Level: 80+

High-level endgame zone filled with Hydras. Extremely strong monsters requiring top-tier gear and strategy.

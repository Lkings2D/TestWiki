üîÆ Enchanting

"Magic rewards patience, not perfection. Every enchantment is a whisper from the beyond."

Enchanting is a mystical crafting skill that imbues gear with magical properties. Unlike blacksmithing, enchanting can never fail‚Äîevery attempt will succeed, though the strength of the enchantment (Level 1, 2, or 3) is determined by chance. No prior crafting skill is required to start, but higher Enchanting Skill Levels greatly increase the odds of stronger enchantments.

üîß How to Enchant

  1. Visit any Magic Shop NPC in major towns and select the Enchant menu.
  2. Select the item to enchant.
  3. Choose your enchantment type from the dropdown menu.
  4. Make sure you have the correct materials and confirm the attempt by hitting the Enchant button.
  5. Receive a guaranteed enchantment! The enchantment level is determined instantly.

Unlike blacksmithing, enchanting has a 100% success rate in applying an enchantment.

However, which level of enchantment you receive is determined by chance, as shown below:

Enchanting weapons will add an elemental damage type. The percentage of the weapon's damage that deals the enchanted element can range from 30-90% depending on the Enchanting Tier achieved.

üõ°Ô∏è Enchanting Gear

Enchanting equipment with an element grants it a percentage-based resistance to that specific element. The percentage resistance your item will provide is dependent on the Enchanting Tier achieved.
